About Tim

Veteran. Public servant. Business leader.

Tim Toerber is a veteran, aviation executive, public servant, husband, father, and outdoor enthusiast running for Lewis County Commissioner, District 3.

Tim served 21 years as a U.S. Army officer and helicopter pilot, including multiple overseas deployments during the Global War on Terrorism with the 160th SOAR. His professional background includes airport operations leadership, Amazon Air leadership, and work as an aviation technology startup executive.

He has also served as Tacoma Narrows Airport Commission Board President and Shore Acres Water Company Board President, working through infrastructure, budgets, maintenance, public service, and ratepayer concerns.

Tim believes county government should be practical, transparent, right-sized, and focused on results.

District Tree Talk

Clear communication matters.

In aviation and the military, the phonetic alphabet exists because clarity matters. “Three” becomes “Tree” so everyone hears the same message the same way.

That same mindset should apply to local government. Tim’s District Tree Talk updates focus on the issues that matter across District 3: public safety, roads, EMS, growth, recreation pressure, infrastructure, stewardship, and county finances.
